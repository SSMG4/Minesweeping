How to Play

  1. Select a difficulty and cell size, then click a tile to begin.
  2. Numbers show how many bombs are adjacent to the cell.
  3. Right-click or long-press to flag a suspected bomb.
  4. Use up to 2 hints per round (click the üí° bulb on the smiley!)
  5. Win by clearing all safe tiles. Lose by clicking a bomb.
  6. Daily challenges and streaks reward regular play!
